About us My name is Adiv Shenkor and I have a degree in film and photography from Sapir College. I came to film studies thanks to my love of photography and

the desire to make my mark in the world through art. Today I want to tell you a little about A. S Photography. Over the years, so many photo apps have been

introduced to our world, some useful and some less so. What is certain is that none of the apps gave meaning to the photos shown. We in the A.S family decided

to make a change and give the world of photography the value it has lost over the years. I present to you A.S Photography World that combines professional

photography with learning, sharing works with the best in the photography industry. We are here to improve the quality of photography in the world, to create

more and more photographers who will love their works and most importantly to give real meaning to photography. "one picture is worth a thousand words".
